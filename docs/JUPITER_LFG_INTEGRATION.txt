THE PROBLEM
LFG has launched 78 projects to a 780k-strong user base through DAO-governed curation [1]. But once a project launches, there's no native vesting infrastructure — team allocations, investor tokens, and community distributions are left for each project to figure out on their own. This creates inconsistency and erodes the trust that LFG's curation process works hard to build.

THE COMPETITION
Competing launchpads are already offering vesting and airdrops as built-in features. Fjord Foundry ($1B+ raised from 100k+ participants) has integrated Sablier to offer token vesting as a native option to its sale creators [2].

LFG can offer the same — or better.

THE SOLUTIONS
Sablier can power native vesting for every LFG launch:
1. Team Vesting — e.g. "🔒 Team tokens: 20% locked, 1-year cliff, 2-year linear"
2. Investor Distribution — e.g. "🔒 Investor tokens vesting linearly over 24 months"
3. Community Airdrops — e.g. "🎁 Claim your airdrop: 500 tokens available"

Schedules are fully configurable per project. All on-chain, verifiable, and visible on the LFG launch page.

THE PROBLEM

LFG's Curation is Strong. Post-Launch Infrastructure is Missing.

The DAO voting process ensures only quality projects make it through — deBridge, Sanctum, and Zeus Network are proof that the model works [3]. But after the DLMM launch event, each project is on its own for token distribution:

- Team tokens need vesting schedules (cliff + linear unlock)
- Investor allocations need transparent, verifiable distribution
- Community airdrops need efficient, scalable delivery
- There's no standardized way for LFG participants to verify any of this

Each project builds custom solutions or uses ad-hoc tools. This creates friction, inconsistency, and trust gaps — the opposite of what LFG's curation process stands for.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
THE SOLUTIONS

1. Team/Founder Vesting
Launched projects configure team vesting as part of the LFG launch flow. Parameters are fully customizable per project:
- Cliff period (e.g., 6 months, 1 year)
- Unlock curve (linear, tranched, or a combination)
- Visible on the LFG launch page

Verifiable on-chain — not just a tokenomics slide, but a cryptographic guarantee.

2. Investor Token Distribution
Projects that raised pre-launch capital can distribute investor tokens through Sablier:
- Configurable vesting terms per investor class
- Cliff + linear, pure linear, or tranched unlocks
- Each investor can track and withdraw unlocked tokens independently

3. Community Airdrops
Efficient, scalable token distribution for community allocations:
- Merkle-based airdrops (up to 1M recipients)
- Claimable directly from the LFG launch page
- Can be instant or vested over time

WHY THIS MATTERS FOR LFG

LFG already differentiates through curation. Adding native vesting infrastructure extends that trust from launch day through the entire token lifecycle.

Without vesting:
"We vetted this project. Good luck with everything after launch."

With vesting:
"We vetted this project. Team tokens are locked. Investor allocations are transparent. Everything is verifiable on-chain."

Fjord Foundry already offers Sablier-powered vesting to its sale creators [2] — with $1B+ raised from 100k+ participants, they saw native vesting as a competitive advantage. LFG is in a position to do the same, and with stronger curation, the result is even more compelling.

WHY NOW

The market is in a downturn. Launch volume is lower, teams are heads-down building. This is the best time to integrate infrastructure like vesting — low pressure, time to iterate, and when the market turns, LFG launches come with vesting as a standard feature from the start.

Projects that build during bear markets capture market share during bull markets.

Why Tokens Fail
Pump.Fun partially addresses liquidity rugs by burning the initial LP tokens upon token graduation to Pump Swap. However, the vast majority of tokens launched on Pump.Fun still die because of:

Holder dumps — No mechanism to verify commitment from creators, whales, the team or community
Secondary LP rugs — Additional liquidity (added manually post-graduation) can be pulled anytime
No engagement tools — Nothing keeps holders engaged after graduation

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
THE SOLUTIONS
1. Token Locks
Any holder locks tokens → Token page shows verified lock badge → Buyers see commitment

Creators prove they won't dump
Whales prove they're diamond hands
Team/advisors show transparent vesting
Lock is an NFT (tradeable if holder wants to exit early)
Unlocks on schedule (linearly, with an optional cliff - or in a timelock fashion)
2. Additional Liquidity Locks
Secondary LPs lock their positions → Badge shows locked liquidity amount and duration

Only applies to liquidity added after graduation (as the initial graduation LP is already burned).
3. Community Rewards
Anyone deposits rewards → Snapshot taken → Community members claim directly from the token page

Keeps token communities engaged post-graduation
Can be targeted to a specific type of community members, e.g:
“Distribute rewards to the users who have bought the token in the last 24h”
“Distribute rewards to the users who have added liquidity in the last 7d”
Can also be used by Pump.Fun, itself, as a means to keep the overall community engaged
e.g. by distributing a small % of the Pump.Fun revenues back to a random token community

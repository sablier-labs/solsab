THE OPPORTUNITY
MetaDAO’s Trust Model + Sablier’s Vesting Layer
Futarchy, the bid wall, and the STAMP contract give MetaDAO the strongest investor protection framework on Solana. MetaDAO can integrate Sablier's existing on-chain products — Lockup (token vesting) and Merkle Airdrops (token distribution) — to add features across both project types:

For Post-Raise (STAMP) projects:
- STAMP-compliant vesting for pre-ICO investors — using Sablier Lockup: 24-month linear unlock, audited, battle-tested, with NFT-based locks that let investors trade their position instead of waiting

For Permissionless projects:
- Voluntary founder/team token locks — using Sablier Lockup: without curation, founders who lock their tokens via Sablier stand out from those who don't. A verified on-chain lock becomes a trust signal for ICO participants.

For both:
- A new "Vesting Bonus" mechanic for ICO participants — using Sablier Lockup: optional, per-project, rewarding investors who commit long-term with bonus tokens
- Ecosystem rewards — using Sablier Merkle Airdrops: reward campaign participation, investment milestones, holding loyalty, and more

Sablier's products are already live on Solana mainnet — MetaDAO integrates them, while Sablier continues expanding and maintaining them. And every vesting withdrawal and reward claim generates fee revenue for MetaDAO — turning each ICO from a one-time event into an ongoing revenue source.

WHAT SABLIER BRINGS
Pre-ICO Investor Vesting (STAMP Compliance)
MetaDAO integrates Sablier Lockup into the ICO flow: STAMP investors' tokens enter a Sablier lock → 24-month linear unlock begins at Delivery Notice → investor withdraws as tokens unlock

- Fully compliant with STAMP's 24-month linear vesting requirement
- Verifiable on-chain: anyone can confirm that investor tokens are properly locked
- Dashboard for investors to track unlocked amounts and withdraw
- Works out of the box for every MetaDAO ICO
- Each lock is an NFT — investors who need early liquidity can sell their locked position on secondary markets instead of waiting or pressuring the project
Founder/Team Token Locks for Permissionless Projects
Without curation, permissionless projects need other ways to signal legitimacy. MetaDAO integrates Sablier Lockup as an optional trust feature for founders:

- Founders lock a portion of their team tokens → MetaDAO ICO page shows a verified lock badge
- Fully voluntary — but projects that lock stand out from those that don't
- Configurable lock duration and unlock curve (e.g. linear, cliff + linear, etc.)
- Each lock is an NFT — tradeable on secondary markets if the founder needs early liquidity
- ICO participants can verify the lock on-chain before committing capital

For permissionless projects, this is the closest equivalent to what STAMP enforces for post-raise projects — but driven by market incentives rather than legal obligation.
 ICO Vesting Bonus (Optional, Per-Project)
A new ICO mechanic MetaDAO could build on top of Sablier Lockup:

- By default, ICO participants receive 100% liquid tokens at TGE (current behavior, unchanged)
- Projects can optionally enable a "Vesting Bonus" — investors who choose to vest a portion of their tokens receive bonus allocation
- Configurable per project, e.g.: "Vest 50% of your tokens for up to 6 months → receive up to 15% more tokens"
- Each ICO investor may even get a toggle to configure their preferred split between liquid and vested tokens
- Creates self-selected alignment: long-term believers get rewarded with more tokens, short-term participants get immediate liquidity
- Reduces day-1 sell pressure organically — without forcing anyone into a lock

This doesn't replace the bid wall. It complements it by giving projects another tool to attract committed holders.
 Ecosystem Rewards
MetaDAO integrates Sablier Merkle Airdrops for reward campaigns to incentivize engagement:

- Campaign participation — e.g. "Reward for users who voted on 5+ proposals this month"
- Investment milestones — e.g. "Reward for users who invested $10k+ via MetaDAO" or "Reward for users who participated in 3+ ICOs"
- Holding loyalty — e.g. "Reward for holders who haven't sold or transferred tokens in 90+ days"
- Project-specific campaigns — each launched project can run its own reward distributions

Claimable directly from the MetaDAO interface.
